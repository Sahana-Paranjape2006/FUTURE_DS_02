# FUTURE_DS_02

**Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard**

**Project Overview**

This project presents an interactive Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard built using Power BI as part of Task 2 of the Future Interns Internship. The objective of the project is to analyze customer behavior and identify the key factors that contribute to customer churn. By visualizing churn patterns and customer segments, the dashboard helps businesses understand retention challenges and make data-driven decisions.

**Objectives**

- Analyze customer churn patterns.
- Identify high-risk customer segments.
- Understand the impact of contract type, payment method, and internet services on churn.
- Provide actionable insights to improve customer retention.

**Tools and Technologies**

- Power BI (Dashboard and Data Visualization)
- Microsoft Excel (Data Cleaning and Preparation)
- DAX (Calculated Measures and KPIs)

**Dashboard Structure**

Page 1: Customer Churn Overview

This page provides a high-level summary of customer churn metrics and segmentation.

**Key components:**

- Total Customers
- Churn Rate
- Retention Rate
- Average Monthly Charges
- Churn Distribution
- Payment Methods Ranked by Churn Risk
- Churn Rate by Contract Type
- Churn Proportion by Senior Citizen Status
- Monthly Charges vs Churn across Internet Services

Page 2: Churn Drivers and Deep Dive

This page focuses on identifying the main drivers of churn and analyzing customer behavior.

**Key components:**

- Customer distribution by Internet Service and Contract Type
- Churn Rate by Customer Tenure Group
- Average Customer Tenure by Churn Status
- Key Influencers visual to identify factors contributing to churn
- Analytical insights and recommendations

**Key Insights**

- Customers with month-to-month contracts have the highest churn rate.
- Electronic check payment method is associated with higher churn risk.
- Customers with shorter tenure are more likely to churn.
- Internet service type and pricing influence customer retention patterns.

**Business Recommendations**

- Encourage customers to move to long-term contracts.
- Improve early-stage customer experience and onboarding.
- Promote automatic payment methods to reduce churn risk.
- Develop targeted retention strategies for high-risk customer segments.

**Learning Outcomes**

Through this project, the following skills were developed:

- Data visualization and dashboard design
- Business data analysis and interpretation
- Identifying key business metrics and KPIs
- Data storytelling and insight generation
